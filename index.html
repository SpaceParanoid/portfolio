About Me

I started learning about web development in middle school (the late 90's days of Geocities and midi files). In high school I was using HTML and CSS in my LiveJournal themes and my first website. I studied Information Technology, Game Development, and eventually completed a graduate program in Library and Information Science before finally realizing - "you've been focusing on everything adjacent to computer science - why didn't you just study Computer Science?" Determined to stay away from more student loan debt, I decided to take the self-learning path and in just 5 months I learned more frontend, backend, and other development concepts listed in the Skills section, including:

  • Javascript
  • React.js
  • Node.js
  • Data Structures
  • Web Architecture
  • Servers

Currently working on: fleshing out my portfolio with different projects and learning new programming languages.
